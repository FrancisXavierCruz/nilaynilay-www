AstroGenesis Revolution

Revolutionary forces struggle to eliminate the tyrant who rules the desert planet Azopaya through military power and monopoly of ipsilin—a liquid resource that the elite use to treat wounds, cure diseases, and extend their lifespans. Elias Dimasalang, a child soldier, is on a mission to stop a rogue comrade from cartwheeling off the slippery slope. To accomplish this, he must learn the difference between being a soldier and being a warrior.
