AstroGenesis Revolution

Wandering about with his dog, an innocent fisher boy tumbles off a cliff and finds himself in a land of despair where he must be crafty in order to survive. He is determined to return home, even if it means turning the world upside-down.

Meanwhile, revolutionary forces struggle to eliminate the tyrant who rules the desert planet through military power and monopoly of ipsilin—a liquid resource that the elite use to treat wounds, cure diseases, and extend their lifespans. Elias Dimasalang, a child soldier, is on a mission to stop a rogue comrade from cartwheeling off the slippery slope. To accomplish this, he must learn the difference between being a soldier and being a warrior.

Aether Paradox

In the land of Alva, a group of young soldiers was tasked to stop a terrorist from killing their council leaders. As their leaders get killed one by one, however, they discover that the people they are trying to protect are not who they seem, and the council they serve hides a terrible past that could destroy the very foundation of their world and everything they were taught to believe in.
